About Me

Hey! I'm ADITYA SHARMA

- |


Passionate Full Stack Developer and Innovator with a strong foundation in programming, embedded systems, Designing and 3D printing.

As a Computer Science student with a deep interest in intelligent systems, I work at the intersection of code and hardware. Using platforms like Raspberry Pi (Zero 2W, 4, 5), ESP32 (DevKit, NodeMCU, S3), Arduino (Mega, UNO, Nano), and Jetson Nano, I build end-to-end solutions that combine embedded computing with real-time decision-making. My experience includes integrating spatial and environmental sensing units, resolving signal integrity challenges, and optimizing PWM-based actuator control. From syncing servo motion to live audio using Whisper AI to designing systems that run offline with low-latency responsiveness, I focus on creating software-driven hardware that is stable, scalable, and ready for real-world deployment.

My foundation in Computer Science extends beyond hardware integration into the core principles of computing—data structures, algorithms, operating systems, computer networks, and software architecture. I’m equally comfortable managing system-level programming on Linux-based environments, optimizing performance at the OS layer, and working with APIs and multithreading for concurrent tasks. I’ve explored AI and NLP concepts through projects involving speech recognition, classification, and text analysis, and I actively experiment with deploying lightweight models on edge devices. My interest in scalable computing has also led me to explore cloud platforms and containerized environments, giving me a holistic view of how intelligent systems operate—from the sensor to the server.

I enjoy building systems that are efficient, responsive, and dependable-whether it’s a real-time robotics project, an AI-powered tool, or a sensor-driven device. I focus on clarity, usability, and performance, ensuring each solution is designed to work seamlessly in practical, offline, and often resource-constrained environments.

I thrive in practical, collaborative environments where ideas are exchanged freely, challenges are tackled together, and learning happens through doing. Working with others allows me to see problems from new perspectives, adapt quickly, and build more thoughtful, effective solutions. For me, collaboration isn't just about teamwork-it's a key part of how I grow and innovate.

I don’t just build things to work - I build them to matter.
